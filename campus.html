1. The building

Lawns, landscaped gardens and playgrounds surround the building, which houses modern classrooms and state-of-the-art laboratories. Terrace gardens and a stylish atrium provide a welcoming and stimulating environment for learning. Colour, space and light combine to enhance the rooms, the corridors and the labs. From its firm foundation, the school building reaches step by step to a height of seven storeys - a reflection of the school's aspiration to provide a sound base from which students will rise to great heights.
The school's laboratories, learning centre, the cafeterias, classrooms and the auditorium have been air-conditioned; as a policy, the air-conditioning facility will be made use of selectively in various facilities depending on the need.

2. classrooms


The School has 60 classrooms on campus. Every classroom has a public address system, digital clocks, display and writing boards, lockers for students, custom made furniture, and a provision for broadband Internet connection, multimedia support and air conditioning.

4. Playground


The adjacent playground which we share with the American School of Bombay includes a grass pitch, tennis and basketball courts, providing opportunity for a range of outdoor sports, measuring about 2.3 acres. The school campus also has 2 small playground for LKG & UKG classes and for the primary classes. There is also a volleyball court within the school campus.

5. Learning Center


The Learning Centre is central to each student's academic progress. In it we have shelf space for some 40,000 books, with already a rich collection. In addition, students have access to periodicals and audio-visual resources, the Internet and photocopying facilities. The Learning Centre is equipped with 18 desktops for reading, reference and research requirement of senior students. The Learning Centre also supports the archiving of the school's various learning resources and records.
