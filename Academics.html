- Nuzha International School located in Mumbai offers a wide array of opportunities to its learners to develop social skills, embrace collaboration and network as one of the best play schools in Mumbai. The environment plays a very important role in doing so and is the chief contributor to a child’s journey of learning. Every child has the innate ability to learn and with a congenial and creative environment, every child’s learning journey becomes a delight at Nahar International preschool.

- In our Early Years children develop outstanding social, language, aesthetic and academic skills through play, music, drama, art and physical education. There are dedicated teachers and learning spaces for children to explore themselves, starting labs to auditoriums, libraries, play area, cafeteria and much more. Each child of every age group receives individual attention and is encouraged to learn, make friends and feel successful.





- In early primary years, the students learn how the past is different from the present. They compare everyday items and buildings of today with items in the past and consider how present life differs to the past. At the end of the primary years, Nahar International School in Mumbai offer a solid groundwork for exploring World History. Students learn about significant periods of time, including a study on the Romans, Ancient Egypt and Medieval History.

- Nuzha International School offers the flexible Cambridge Secondary 1 curriculum in Middle School (Grades 6 - 8), for each subject - English, Mathematics and Science, providing a clear teaching structure. For other subjects, the IGCSE curriculum is used to plan the Middle School curriculum. Regular Progression Tests are conducted during the school year to assess students’ learning and performance and to report progress to learners and parents.

-The school offers a stimulating curriculum that lets learners build inquisitiveness and curiosity. Highly focused on the overall development of students, there are 70 subjects under the Cambridge International IGCSE curriculum, divided under subject groups like English, Technology, Science, Humanities, Language, Arts, Social Science and Mathematics.



It is mandatory to choose at least one subject from each group.
